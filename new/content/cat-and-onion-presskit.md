## Description

A warm and whimsical cat adventure where you climb, talk, fly and smack your way to places where no cat has ever gone before! Go on a short yet cosmic journey packed with curious characters, imaginative environments and good vibes.

## Release dates and platforms

### March 12, 2024 on Steam

Windows, Mac, Steam Deck and Linux https://store.steampowered.com/app/2781210/CAT__ONION/

## Developed by

**Zylinski Games**. Solo indie game developer Karl Zylinski. Has made all the code, graphics, music, sound and marketing for CAT & ONION. Based in Sweden.
